    Admissions

    AGE AND ACADEMIC CRITERIA FOR ADMISSIONS

    • A student must be 3 years old by June 1 to be eligible to enroll in the Nursery. He or she must show emotional readiness, that is, be left alone by the parents for a couple of hours to attend his class.
    • A student must be 6 years old by June 1 to be eligible to enroll in first grade. He / she must be able to read and write.
    • A student will be admitted to Grades 2 to Fourth Year High School on the basis of successful completion of the previous grade and satisfactory scores on the admissions examinations.
    • A student who does not pass the entrance examination may be admitted on a probationary status and is required to undergo tutorial or remedial classes.

    A. Admission for Old Students

    Old Students Requirements:

    • Clearance Form
    • F-138 (Report Card) Original and 1 photocopy

    Registration Procedure:

    • Proceed to the Assessment Room and present the recent Report Card and clearance form to get the following:
      • Registration form
      • List of books
      • Uniform Order form
      • List of other requirements
    • Complete the registration forms and submit to the teacher-in-charge for assessment of tuition fees.
    • Proceed to the Cashier in Windows 1 or 2 to pay tuition, books as per list, and uniforms.
    • Proceed to the Bookstore and present receipt to claim paid books and uniforms.
    • Purchase other requirements as per list in other Bookstores.

    B. Registration Procedures for New Students

    New Students Requirements:

    • F-138 (Report Card) original and 1 photocopy
    • Birth Certificate (photocopy only)
    • Recommendation letter from former school
    • 1 long brown envelope

    Registration Procedures:

    • Present requirements to Admin Office in Window 1 and arrange for an entrance test.
    • Proceed to the cashier to pay Entrance Exam Fee of P400.
    • Present receipt to Testing Room to take entrance exam.
    • Wait for the result of the entrance exam and book for an interview conducted in the Testing Room.
    • Proceed to the Assessment Room. Present Entrance Test Results and submit requirements to get the following:
      • Registration form
      • List of books
      • Uniform order form
      • List of other requirements
    • After completing registration, submit to teacher-in-charge for assessment of tuition fees.
    • Proceed to the Cashier in Windows 1 or 2 to pay tuition, books as per list, and uniforms.
    • Proceed to the Bookstore and present receipt to claim paid books and uniforms.
    • Purchase other requirements as per list in other Bookstores.

    C. Registration Procedures for Foreign Students

    Transferees from Abroad Requirements:

    • F-138 (Report Card) original and photocopy
    • F-137 or Transcript of Records
    • Birth Certificate (photocopy only)
    • Recommendation letter from the former school
    • Alien Certification of Registration (ACR) and Special Study Permit
    • 1 long brown envelope

    Registration Procedure:

    • Present the Alien Certification of Registration (ACR) and Special Study Permit.
    • Present all credentials (forms evaluated and duly approved by the Department of Education), F-138 and F-137 to be evaluated by the Asst. Principal for appropriate grade/year level as per Philippine Standard of Education.
    • Arrange with the Admin. Office to take the Entrance Examination.
    • Wait for the result of the Entrance Exam. Afterwards, secure a schedule for an interview.
    • After passing the evaluation test and entrance examination and interview, secure enrollment form from the Assessment Room.
    • After completing the forms, have it assessed for payment required upon enrollment.
    • Proceed to the Business Office for payment, including books and uniforms.
    • Present receipt and list of books and materials and claim them from the bookstore.
