K-12 PROGRAMS

PRESCHOOL DEPARTMENT

The FCS Preschool department offers online classes, activities, and play for students aged 3 ½ to 5 years old to develop their communication, reading, writing, counting, socialization, exploration, and artistic skills. At a young age, the students are taught how to take care of themselves, their families, and friends inside and outside their homes. The core attitudes being developed among FCS preschoolers are kindness, confidence, responsibility, creativity, and most of all, Christlikeness.

ELEMENTARY DEPARTMENT

The FCS Elementary department offers online classes, activities, and performance tasks to develop students’ fluency in communication, reading comprehension, creative and academic writing, mathematical reasoning, artistic and musical abilities, interpersonal skills, physical skills, and Christian behaviour. Teachers and parents work hand in hand to nurture each student’s identity and sense of responsibility towards their classmates, their immediate communities, and their environment. The core attributes being developed among FCS elementary students are leadership, honesty, sportsmanship, resourcefulness, responsibility, kindness, academic excellence and most of all, Christlikeness.

JUNIOR HIGHSCHOOL DEPARTMENT

The FCS Junior High School Department offers online classes with a wide array of activities inclusive of well drafted performance tasks particularly Thesis Writing with oral defense, research, selected organizations that hone the students’ leadership skills, effective communication, academic and creative writing, public speaking, mathematical reasoning, technology literacy, critical thinking, decision making, and Christian character. The musical recital is not a mere adherence to Applied music which was prescribed by the DepEd, but is highly instrumental to develop great love for music writing and playing various instruments. Students are facilitated to seize their aptitude, inclinations, skills and talents and further prime up their full potentials, develop their own identities, strengthen bonds well with their peers and to grow towards God’s calling for them as they transition into young adults and young members of the Filipino community. FCS Junior High School students are trained to be academically competent, morally responsible, compassionate, and most of all Christlike.

SENIOR HIGHSCHOOL DEPARTMENT

The FCS Senior High School Department offers online classes, activities, workshops and trainings, webinars, organisations, community engagement and research to develop students’ skills, abilities, values and Christian philosophy. These are needed for them to apply their learning in real life situations, find solutions and solve problems that are present in the community and the world. They are exposed to tasks that will take them to their desired professions through the academic strands - STEM, HUMSS and ABM. Here at FCS Senior High School, STEM offers Technical Drawing and AutoCad for the students who are interested in taking up engineering and architecture courses in college. Students are given the opportunities and the assistance to equip and prepare them with essential knowledge and skills towards the chosen path in the higher level of education, future employment or entrepreneurship as beacons of God’s love for all. FCS Senior High School students are given the independence and tools they need to grow and mature as they pursue God’s plan for them.
